Step 1

From your shopify admin click on Apps. Click on customize your store
Step 2

The app store opens up on a new page.You can also access the app store directly by visiting https://apps.shopify.com/. If you visit the app store using the URL, be sure to click “log in” in the top right hand corner and enter the login details to store where you want to add an app. Type our app name in search box at the top. Now click on add app button on app detail page. You are redirect back to your shopify admin to authorize the use of app in your store. From there, Click “Install app”. After you install an app,it appears in the apps section of your shopify admin Click on app name and Add access key and the field URL, then click on submit button.
